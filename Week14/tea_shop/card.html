Store Card Concept

Our store card concept blends history, geography, and cultural elements to create unique designs for each location. Each card is a work of art that showcases the cultural characteristics and brand story of its respective store.

We believe every store deserves its own distinctive design. Through careful visual design and cultural integration, we create a cultural bridge between our brand and customers. Every card use becomes a gateway to cultural discovery.

Taipei Xinyi Store - Modern Heritage

Inspired by Taipei's contemporary architecture and cultural depth. The design blends Taipei 101's towering presence with traditional Eastern aesthetics, showcasing urban cultural fusion in Xinyi.

Hsinchu Seaside Store - Coastal Charm

Drawing from Hsinchu's coastal landscapes. Fresh ocean blues and sandy golds intertwine, conveying the tranquility and freedom of the seaside. Wave patterns symbolize the geographic character of Hsinchu.

Yilan Mountain Store - Nature's Beauty

Inspired by Yilan's mountain and water scenery. Deep greens and lake blues represent the magnificent views of Lanyang Plain. Designs incorporate Turtle Mountain and Snow Mountain silhouettes.

Taichung Temple Store - Religious Heritage

Drawing from Taichung's temple culture. Traditional religious aesthetics merge with modern design techniques, presenting the intricate and respectful essence of temple art in each card.

Tainan Ancient City Store - Historical Richness

Themed around Tainan's rich historical heritage. Muted tones paired with historic monument patterns convey Tainan's deep cultural significance as Taiwan's historical center.

Kaohsiung Harbor Store - Urban Vitality

Inspired by Kaohsiung's harbor scenery. Modern harbor lines combined with ocean blues showcase the international port city's energy and openness. Features elements of the Ferris wheel and 85 Sky Tower.
